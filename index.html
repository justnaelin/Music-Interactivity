Welcome to Super Pretty Music Interaction

Super Pretty Music Interaction allows the user to play with music in a fun, visually-appealing, way. Users can click on tiles to play snippets of the song corresponding with that tile. This works by pushing a song into an array and associating each index of the array with a tile in the pattern. Other functionality include trailing through the song in anyway the user pleases with the arrow keys, selecting a tile and playing the song from that bar, and incrementing and decrementing the beats to be skipped.
